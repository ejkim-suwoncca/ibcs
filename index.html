Club History

FC Barcelona was founded in 1899 and is one of the most successful football clubs in the world. The club represents Catalan pride and is famous for its beautiful attacking football style.


Barcelona has won numerous trophies including La Liga titles, Copa del Rey trophies, and multiple UEFA Champions League championships.


Their legendary stadium Camp Nou is one of the most iconic football stadiums in the world.
